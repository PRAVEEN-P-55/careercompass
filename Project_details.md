# CAREERCOMPASS: ONE-STOP PERSONALIZED CAREER & EDUCATION ADVISOR

---

## 1. PROJECT TITLE

**CareerCompass: One-Stop Personalized Career & Education Advisor**

---

## 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

The decline in student enrollment in government degree colleges is not a consequence of poor infrastructure or lack of teaching resources. At the heart of the problem lies a critical gap in awareness — most students and often their parents do not understand the importance of graduation, what career opportunities different degree courses unlock, or how to choose a subject stream based on personal interest and future prospects.

After passing Class 10 or 12, students face confusion about which subject stream (Arts, Science, Commerce) to opt for, what degree programs are available in nearby government colleges, what jobs or higher studies they can pursue after a particular course, and whether pursuing graduation is even worth the effort when short-term job or skill-based courses seem more accessible.

This lack of clarity leads to poor academic decisions, dropouts, or migration to private institutions that often promise but fail to deliver quality education.

---

## 3. PROJECT OBJECTIVES

### Primary Objectives

- Develop a personalized digital guidance platform for students after Class 10 and 12.
- Provide aptitude and interest-based course and stream suggestions.
- Map degree courses to career paths, government exams, and higher education options.
- List nearby government colleges with available programs, cut-offs, and facilities.
- Send timely notifications for admissions, scholarships, and counseling schedules.
- Deliver AI-driven personalized recommendations for courses, colleges, and career paths.

### Secondary Objectives

- Reduce student dropout rates after Class 10 and 12.
- Improve enrollment in government degree colleges.
- Empower students with access to reliable, localized career guidance.
- Strengthen the perception of government colleges as viable career-building institutions.
- Provide access to open-source study materials and scholarship information.

## 7. EXPECTED OUTCOME

The proposed CareerCompass platform will serve as a one-stop personalized career and education advisor for students after Class 10 and 12. By leveraging aptitude assessments, AI-powered recommendations (via Antigravity), and a comprehensive government college directory, the platform will empower students to make informed academic decisions.

The system will help reduce dropout rates, improve enrollment in government degree colleges, and provide equitable access to career guidance for students in rural and semi-urban areas. Through real-time notifications, scholarship alerts, and study material access, CareerCompass will ensure no student is left behind due to a lack of awareness or guidance.

---

## 8. IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

- **Stakeholder Collaboration**: Involve government education departments, school teachers, NGOs, and counselors for content and outreach.
- **Technology Development**: Build a scalable, lightweight app with offline features for poor internet areas.
- **Pilot Launch**: Start in one or two districts with low college enrollment; gather feedback from students and teachers.
- **Full-Scale Rollout**: Roll out state-wide or nationally through government schools and skill centers.
- **Monitoring & Feedback**: Real-time analytics to track usage, recommendation accuracy, and successful transitions to college enrollment.
